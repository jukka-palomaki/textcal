About the Text Cal app

Text Cal shows Google Calendar events in text mode (read-only) including all event details. No need to open each event separately which can save your time. The time span is three weeks.

You can also copy easily the text if you select the Simple Text mode in the top right corner.

By tapping your account bar you can select other calendars that are visible to your google account.

You can navigate to future or past one week a time by using buttons at the top.

Refreshing works by swiping down.
